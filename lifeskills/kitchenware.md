### Materials
 * 18/10, 18/8 and 18/0 are the percentages of Chromium and Nickel alloys in stainless steel. Generally, more the nickel, the more corrosion resistant and the more durable the steel. Chromium provides prevents rusting.

### Knives
#### Materials used
##### High Carbon
Low Chromium
###### Pros
 * sharp edges
 * easy to sharpen
###### Cons
 * reacts easily with acidic foods like onions, tomatoes and lemons causing rust

##### Stainless steel
Chromium > 13%
###### Pros
 * corrosion resistance
 * good balance of toughness, durablility and sharpness
###### Cons
 * harder to sharpen
 * not as strong as high carbon

##### Damuscus Steel
Combines high carbon and stainless steel
###### Pros
 * high performance + corrosion resistance
 * aesthetic
###### Cons
 * expensive
